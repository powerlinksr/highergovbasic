AI Summary: Background
 The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Environmental Management (EM) Portsmouth/Paducah Project Office (PPPO) is issuing a presolicitation notice for the Portsmouth Infrastructure Support Services (ISS) procurement. This contract aims to provide essential infrastructure support services at the Portsmouth site, replacing the current contract awarded to North Wind Dynamics, which is set to expire on December 18, 2026.

Work Details
 The contractor will be responsible for a wide range of infrastructure support services at PORTS, including but not limited to:

- Waste Management
- Environment Safety, Health, and Quality (ESH&Q) Program
- Safeguards and Security (S&S)
- Telecommunications
- Surveillance and Maintenance of Facilities
- Custodial Services
- Grounds Maintenance
- Snow Removal
- Pest Control
- Roadway Parking and Lot Maintenance
- Fleet Management
- Real Property Management
- Records Management and Document Control
- Engineering Services
- Training Services
- Mail Services
- Pollution Prevention
- Other activities supporting the DOE.

Place of Performance
 The principal place of performance will be at the US DOE Portsmouth Gaseous Diffusion Plant located at 3930 US Route 23 South, Piketon, Ohio 45661-0700.
